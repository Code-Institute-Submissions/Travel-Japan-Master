Why Japan?

Japan is an amazing tourist destination with something special to offer everyone. It has a perfect blend of Eastern culture with stunning shines and temples in abundance and Western contemporary culture, making it an incredibly unique country to explore that you will undoubtedly fall in love with. Although a small island, Japan has many vibrant and energetic cities for you to explore. From Tokyo to Osaka, there are seemingly endless amounts of quirky karaoke bars, delicious eateries, and fun arcades to immerse yourself in.

The Views

Japan is filled to the brim with the most amazing sights. Whether you are in one of the busiest cities or in a more remote part of the country, one of the things you will notice is the beautiful shrines and temples which seem to be dotted around on almost every corner (Kyoto alone is home to 1600 Buddhist temples and 400 Shinto shrines!). There is seemingly endless natural landscapes for you to discover all over Japan, which will easily leave you feeling overwhelmed. For the more adventurous, a hike up Mount Fuji to see the sunrise is definitely something you won’t forget, or a visit to Nara park to see the huge Buddha statue and be greeted by the friendly deer who will happily eat out of your hands.

Attraction of the Month

The Ueno Summer Festival hosts many different events, from the Ueno Natsu Matsuri Parade, to a lantern floating festival.
The main highlight of this festival is the Ueno Natsu Matsuri Parade, this parade features a wide array of dancers each with different styles, from Yosakoi to Samba. This 5 week event, beggining in July, is not to be missed by any traveller!
